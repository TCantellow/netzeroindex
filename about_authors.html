This project is a product of a collaboration between researchers in the University of Bristol's School of Geographical Sciences.

Tom Cantellow is a post-submission PhD student who specializes in spatial and statistical analysis of politics and economics. He can be contacted here.

Dr Ed Atkins is a Senior Lecturer in Human Geography. His work focuses on energy transitions and socio-economic justice. He can be contacted here.

Cait Robinson is a Senior Research Fellow and Proleptic Lecturer who is currently leading a UKRI Future Leaders Fellowship project mapping ambient vulnerabilities in UK cities.

Sean Fox is an Associate Professor in Global Development focusing on the causes and consequences of global urbanization, the political economy of urban governance, and sustainable city futures.
