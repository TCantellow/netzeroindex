This project is a product of a collaboration between researchers in the University of Bristol's School of Geographical Sciences.

The research team:

  • Tom Cantellow: A researcher specialising in spatial and statistical analysis of politics and economics. Contact him here.
  • Dr. Ed Atkins: A Senior Lecturer in Human Geography focusing on energy transitions and socio-economic justice. Contact him here.
  • Dr. Sean Fox: An Associate Professor in Global Development, focusing on the causes and consequences of global urbanization, the political economy of urban governance, and sustainable city futures. Contact him here.
  • Dr. Cait Robinson: A Senior Research Fellow and Proleptic Lecturer, researching the causes and consequences of different types of spatial inequality, with a particular interest in energy poverty and energy justice. Contact her here.
