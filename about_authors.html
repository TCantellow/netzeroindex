This project is a product of a collaboration between researchers in the University of Bristol's School of Geographical Sciences.

The research team:

  • Dr Tom Cantellow: A researcher specialising in spatial and statistical analysis of politics and economics.
  • Dr Ed Atkins: A Senior Lecturer in Human Geography focusing on energy transitions and socio-economic justice.
  • Professor Sean Fox: A Professor in Global Development, focusing on the causes and consequences of global urbanization, the political economy of urban governance, and sustainable city futures.
  • Dr Cait Robinson: A Senior Research Fellow and Proleptic Lecturer, researching the causes and consequences of different types of spatial inequality, with a particular interest in energy poverty and energy justice.
