The Net Zero Vulnerability Index

Interactive Map About the Research About the Authors PolicyBristol Report

This project is a product of a collaboration between researchers in the University of Bristol's School of Geographical Sciences>. Tom Cantellow is a post-submission PhD student who specialises in spatial and statistical analysis of politics and economics, Dr Ed Atkins is a Senior Lecturer who works on the contested character of environmental and energy policy and politics, Cait Robinson, is a Senior Research Fellow and Proleptic Lecturer who is currently leading a UKRI Future Leaders Fellowship project mapping ambient vulnerabilities in UK cities. and Sean Fox is an Associate Professor focusing primarily on the causes and consequences of global urbanization, the political economy of urban governance, and sustainable city futures.
