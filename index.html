Net Zero Index

Welcome to the Net Zero Interactive Map

This interactive map presents data for Local Authority Districts (LADs), Local Enterprise Partnerships (LEPs), and Combined Authorities (CAs) in the context of net zero emissions:

  • LADs: Local Authority Districts representing regional governance areas.
  • LEPs: Voluntary partnerships between local authorities and businesses.
  • CAs: Groups of local authorities collaborating on strategic functions.

Special thanks to the contributors to this project: Tom Cantellow (author), Ed Atkins, Cait Robinson, and Sean Fox.
